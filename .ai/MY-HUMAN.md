Always verify mobile-first when testing responsive designs and page rendering.

Follow the KISS principle. Easier to maintain code is better than obfuscated code. If you have to choose between the two, choose easier to read and maintain code.

Avoid UTF-8 characters in documentation. Colored circles are OK for emphasis. Don't use emdashes or the superfluous words like "entirely" unless they add meaning.

Focus on clarity and conciseness. Use simple language. Break down complex concepts into small digestible pieces.
